Is it necessary to get implants under the muscle?
Sub muscular implant benifits
Apr 3, 2016
I prefer submuscular biplane implant placement over subglandular placement.  The benefits of submuscular placement are more soft tissue coverage to minimize palpability of the implant, lower capsular contracture rates and a better mammogram following submuscular implants.  Implant movement with muscle action is usually minimal.  I have many patients who compete in fitness competitions and they have no problems with submuscular implants. 
Do I have tubular breasts? Do I need to lose weight before my breast augmentation consult?
Are my breasts tubular
Apr 1, 2016
You do not have tubular breasts.  You do have a wide breast base diameter so I would recommend  choosing a larger implant which will match your base diameter.  A smaller implant might exaggerate the gap between your breasts.  I'm sure you and your surgeon will come up with a good plan.  Your case is pretty straight forward.
