Will a band help lower the implant?
Jun 22, 2020
Generally, implants can take up to a year to fall into appropriate position. It is not uncommon for one to sometimes fall faster than the other. If it has been over a year it is unlikely to change any further and discussing this with your Plastic Surgeon is important. If it’s been less than three months, massage and a strap could help significantly. Good luck.
