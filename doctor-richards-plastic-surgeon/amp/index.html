Do I have tubular breasts? Do I require a lift or can I get a breast augmentation with an areola
Breast lift with implants
May 18, 2020
In order to create a "perkier" look as well as reduce the size of your areola, a lift will be needed. To get you more upper pole fullness and projection, an implant should be placed. You are a great candidate for a breast lift with implants. The scar will go around your areola and down the breast like a lollipop.
Can I get a lift with existing implants? Would bigger implants help the minor sagging appearance
Breast lift on top of existing implants
May 17, 2020
You can definitely have a lift over your existing implants. This is a procedure I frequently perform and is performed in our own state of the art surgical center. Recovery is easier than a breast augmentation. 
Borderline lift? Getting away with just implants?
Breast augmentation with implants
Apr 17, 2020
Implants will improve your cleavage and upper pole fullness. I recommend using silicone implants placed under the muscle through a small breast crease incision. You don't have to have a lift, and should you want to get one later on, that is always a possibility to do a lift over the implants. I hope this helps.
i would like to get a BA, and a grade 3 inverted nipple repair, is it more convenient to do both
Breast augmentation with inverted nipple repair
Mar 15, 2020
These 2 procedures can be done together. Best to contact a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss specifics. For implant sizing, we use a combination of 3D imaging as well as sizers provided by the implant manufacturers to help choose the implants size. 
21 y/o wanting bigger boobs, do I need a lift?
Breast augmentation with or without lift
Mar 9, 2020
You could go either way with your body type. A lift will raise the nipple and areola position to a more youthful spot. Implants will make your breasts larger. Best to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon.

Breast implants without a lift

Definitely high profile

At that size a high profile will better fit your chest measurements. It will definitely give a faker look as well. The implants will provide upper pole fullness. Looks as if you have a fair amount of breast tissue, so you could possibly go above or below the muscle. Saline implants can be overfilled so that the shape of the implants maintain more fullness up top. Another option is to go with one of the more cohesive silicone implants such as a the inspira soft touch (natrelle) or mentor ...
