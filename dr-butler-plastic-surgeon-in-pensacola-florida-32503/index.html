If you lose weight after a BBL will the butt still be bigger than it originally was?
If you lose weight after a BBL will the butt still be bigger than it originally was?
Apr 2, 2020
Hello, thank you for your question !We recommend being at a stable weight preoperatively, as weight fluctuations can alter your result. Weight gain/loss is caused by individual fat cells growing larger or smaller; fat cells do not "disappear" with normal weight loss. Therefore, if you were to have more fat cells in you buttocks than you did before, then yes, it would be bigger than it was when you last weighed 115 pounds if you were to lose weight again.
