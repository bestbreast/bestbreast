Skin excess
Jul 7, 2020
Liposuction does not remove excess skin or tighten the abdominal muscle which I think you need. A tummy tuck does those well and is the procedure for you. Good Luck!
Would I be better off doing the full tummy tuck or just the pannis?
Tummy tuck for sure
Jul 7, 2020
You would benefit from muscle tightening and contouring not just a panus excision. What you want is the best cosmetic outcome. Good luck!

We love insorb staples! I also agree that closure of scarpa's fascia and deep dermal closure is important. Thus, A 3 layer closure. We also use internal Quilting sutures to reduce drain usage.

Your fat removed may be placed in Buttocks or breast or even in other areas to enhance your outcome. I hope this helps, good luck!

Are my implants dropping normally?
Wait 3 months but likely need revision
Mar 21, 2020
There is no rush to have revision surgery now. It may be needed but the best thing IMHO is to use a downward compressson strap and wait 3 months. Are you a patient who has lost a lot of weight prior to surgery? It appears that way and in my experience this in itself makes the procedure more difficult and revision surgery more likely.   It is so hard to wait but the safest option for now. Good Luck.
What would you suggest for severe breast asymmetry? I have multiple questions
A staged approach
Mar 21, 2020
This is a complex level of asymmetry and will be very hard to correct with one operation. Your goal picture is a very nice result but did not likely have your level of asymmetry to start. I would do a reduction on the larger size and tissue expander on the smaller side as the first procedure. The second procedure would be 4 months later to replace the tissue expander on the the smaller side and silicone breast implants placed in both breasts. Good Luck!
Is a fleur de lis my only option?

Could I get a tummy tuck with liposuction if I'm 5ft 6 and weigh 100kg?
Possible body lift VS etended TT
Feb 29, 2020
The best result will likely be a circumferential body lift and or an extended tummy tuck with 360 degree liposuction. Just a routine TT will not remove the skin or fat from the waist and flank areas. I hope this helps, Good Luck!
Do I need a lift? Size recommendations?

Feb 29, 2020
The 10cm distance should be 7-8 cm for a C cup. A implant alone will further stretch you skin and lead to a poor result. Scars while not wanted by most patients will give you the result and longevity you desire. Good Luck!

Based on your picture I think your goals would best be addressed with a breast lift alone. A smaller lifted breast that is perkier is a reasonable goal! Good Luck!

What procedure do you recommend to have perkier and more rounded and firm breasts with smaller are
Breast lift first
Feb 29, 2020
You have significant breast volume and to be smaller and perkier I would think a circumvertical style breast lift would be a great option.
