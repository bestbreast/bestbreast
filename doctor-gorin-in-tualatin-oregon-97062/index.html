Am I beginning to get symmastia?
Not synmastia at this point.
Jul 27, 2015
A true synmastia would have the skin of the sternum lifted off to varying degrees. I do not see that in your case. However, as you have seen, there can be substantial changes after having babies. My suggestion would be to wait until all the babies are done with breast feeding. At that point you would then consider a revision with implants behind the muscle. This can help minimize the synmastia appearance as well as the rippling.
