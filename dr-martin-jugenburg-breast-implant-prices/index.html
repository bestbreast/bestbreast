Breast Augmentation Age Limit?

There are no upper age restrictions on breast augmentation (FDA does have minimum age recommendations for the use of breast implants. Breast augmentation can be safely and successfully performed in individuals your age and older.

A better determinant of operative candidacy is overall health rather than age. The optimal patient is in good health, has reached a stable health weight and has realistic goals for the procedure.

A few age related considerations:

-Skin quality changes with age and some consideration of skin elasticity should be made
-Healing is slowed as we age and thus older patients can expect slight increases in recovery time
-Before any breast surgery it is often prudent to undergo baseline breast imaging.

The risk of development of breast disease increases with age. Screening mammogram pre-op should be considered. As always, discuss your concerns with a board certified plastic surgeon.

Capsular contracture

Capsular contracture is a phenomenon that results in firm breasts due to unusually thick scar tissue. The treatment is surgery .

Best options for implants in athletic women

There are general guidelines for selecting over vs. under, relating primarily to coverage of the implant. In women with low body fat and small breasts, this usually means going under the muscle. However, there are problems with under the muscle in athletic women, especially a tendency for the muscle to distort the breast with flexion.

That is probably why your surgeon recommended over, though the trade-off is a less natural look. There are other options for athletic women such as the subfascial and split muscle technique.

Will Breast Implants Worsen Uneven Breasts?

The vast majority of women have asymmetric breasts. Increasing the breast size by the same exact amount with identical implants does not correct the asymmetry and in some cases it appears worse. By using different size implants your breast as symmetry may be narrowed, be eliminated or if the implant is larger than the difference between the breasts the smaller breast can become the larger breast.

The best way for you to pick implants is to see a Plastic surgeon who offers 3D imaging such as the Canfield VECTRA system which would let you SEE how each implant may look in your particular breast.

This would also allow you to pick the best implants for your asymmetry and those that match your cosmetic vision for your breasts
