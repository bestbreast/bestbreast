Do I really need to wear the uncomfortable breast band?
Breast Aug Band Use
Oct 31, 2018
Thank you for your question. We usually don't recommend using a bandeau or strap until 1 week post-op to allow the incision to heal completely. In your case, there's no need to wear the strap or bandeau if you are happy with where the implants are right now. We only advise wearing a bandeau until you are comfortable with where the implants are sitting/ how it looks. Over time, gravity will cause the implants to drop anyway. I hope this is helpful.

Unilateral Buccal Fat Removal

Hives after Facelift
Oct 23, 2018
After 8 months, it is extremely unlikely that there would be any relationship to your facelift and the hives that you are now experiencing. I would look closely at medications (both topical and oral), new cosmetics or cleansers, laundry detergents, etc. A dermatologist visit may be very helpful to you.

How do I find the right doctor for tubular breast augmentation?
Tubular Breast Surgeon
Oct 22, 2018
Tubular breasts are a not so uncommon occurrence. The shape is markedly improved with breast augmentation. There are a few technical issues that need to be addressed during the surgery to get a good result. Most board certified plastic surgeons that do breast augmentation as significant part of their practice should be familiar with the treatment required for tubular breasts.

What is the cause of the hardening I have under my breast?
Breast hardness
Oct 18, 2018
It appears you had a mastopexy with an "anchor" incision. It is very common to have hardening under the breast after this procedure and it is most likely due to scar tissue from the surgery. This should soften over time. Massage can help. Very unlikely, though  possible, there could be fat or breast necrosis from lack of blood supply to the lower breast tissues after the surgery. If so, this may also soften with time.

Why is my ear still numb four weeks after facelift? Both ears have changed shape and lobe is l
Ear Shape After Facelift
Jul 20, 2018
It is still too early to make a determination about your ear shape. The swelling of the earlobes is the last area of swelling to resolve after a facelift. It can take as long as a couple of months. The numbness of the ear is likely due to stretching of the greater auricular nerve from the procedure and almost always resolves after a few months as well.
Pain in breast when sneezing or coughing
Breast Pain
Jul 18, 2018
It sounds like you have developed costochondritis which is an inflammation around the ribs and occurs more frequently with breast implants. It is often brought on by a long episodes of coughing or any activity that increases the activity of the chest area...like a new exercise program. You should put the area at rest. NSAID's (Advil) can also help. It should resolve in a few weeks. If it persists or worsens you should contact your MD to confirm that nothing else is causing your symptoms.
