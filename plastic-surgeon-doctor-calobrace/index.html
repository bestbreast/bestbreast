Breast Implant Revision
Aug 17, 2018

Breast lift and augmentation, are these stitches really thick or is it the tape?
Mastopexy Recovery
Aug 17, 2018
Hello. It appears that it is just blood, adhesive residual and/or bruising. Looks like you are healing normally. The incision line will change as swelling decreases and as your deep dissolvable sutures start dissolving. Steri-strips are important in the first weeks after surgery. Using scar treatment twice daily is also important  2-3 weeks into your recovery. Make sure to follow-up with your surgeon and follow their recommendations. Happy healing!

Waxing after Dysport
Jul 12, 2018

Will Cellfina work on loose leg skin?
Cellfina
Jun 6, 2018
Cellfina is a highly effective treatment for dimpling in the buttocks and thighs caused from fibrous banding.  However, Cellfina does not tighten skin and does not reduce fat.  Many of my Cellfina patients benefit from non-invasive skin tightening following the Cellfina procedure.  Because different offices have various ways of tightening skin, I recommend an in person consultation before proceeding with a treatment option to ensure expectations align.
