Rhinoplasty after Accutane
Jan 15, 2019

Follow-Up Question: Why do some surgeons insist on doing cosmetic surgery at a hospital?
Cosmetic surgery at surgery center or hospital?
Jan 15, 2019
Most cosmetic surgery is performed at surgery centers. It is easier to control costs at a surgery center and it is more appropriate for outpatient surgery. For insurance cases, sometimes the insurer will not pay for surgery centers and will require surgery to be performed at a hospital. Also, for high risk cases, a hospital may be more appropriate.
Could menopause cause a sudden neck change?
Menopause and facial aging
Jan 15, 2019
It is common to see diminishing skin elasticity when patients go through menopause. It may seem sudden. Typically a neck lift or laser resurfacing are the best options to combat this normal type of aging. Good luck.
