Will I regret it if I just get breast implants with no lift?
Augment or Lift Augment
Dec 11, 2018
You appear to have a grade 3 ptosis, which means your nipples are well below the inframammary crease. No matter what is done, you will need a lift. Large High Profile implants will just give you a large mass above the nipples which will then appear to be falling off the breast. Better to face reality and consider your lift options than get the implants alone and be unhappy. The scars fade with time. Hope this helpsDr. J

Bottoming Out:
Dec 7, 2018
Bottoming out can occur at this early if the inframammary crease was violated or the pectoralis muscle is too close to the crease. Typically it takes several weeks for the implant to drop and even months before they are in their final place. If you feel her implants are too low at this time we should bring it up to your surgeon so that corrective measures can be planned.
