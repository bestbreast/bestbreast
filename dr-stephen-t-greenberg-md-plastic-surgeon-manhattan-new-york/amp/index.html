Should I get a saline or silicone breast augmentation?
Saline implants vs silicone implants
Jul 7, 2020
Thank you for your question.  I personally recommend the newer silicone gel implants.  They consist of cohesive silicone so the no longer leak like silicone used to many years ago. They tend to look and feel very natural and could be yours for a lifetime. Saline implants are great but they need to be replaced every 10 years and you can also see the defect of the implant.  I would suggest speaking with your Board Certified Plastic Surgeon in person to talk about your options.  Best of luck ...
I smoke 3 to 4 cigarettes a day, is it safe for me to get a breast augmentation?
Smoking before surgery
Jul 7, 2020
Thank you for your question.  I typically recommend all patients to stop smoking a minimum of 4-6 weeks before surgery and after surgery.  Smoking can interfere with healing and scarring.  Do I turn patients away if they do not stop? Not necessarily depending on the situation, but I make sure they understand they are at a higher risk for healing issues.  I would suggest speaking with your surgeon of choice and following their recommendations.  
How long does it take to recover after breast augmentation?
Breast Augmentation recovery
Jul 7, 2020
Thank you for your question.  Each surgeon will have their own protocol they wish their patients to follow.  I suggest 4 weeks of no heavy lifting or working out.  With that being said I do think 3 weeks is enough time and I would suggest having a extra set of hands when having to lift any heavy boxes, trays etc.  I would recommend speaking with your Board Certified Plastic Surgeon as following their recommendations.  Best of luck to you.
