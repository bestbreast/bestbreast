Being a 34AA - want a full and natural look. Should I go with 450cc or 500cc?
Achieving a natural result
Mar 18, 2015
Given the diameter of your chest, using a high profile implant may be your best option to achieve a full breast size. The base of your breast will determine the best size implant that your chest would accommodate. Placing a very large implant in a breast with a small base diameter is potentially going to lead to complications such as synmastia (breasts too close together with loss of cleavage plane) and bottoming out (implant falling below the natural breast crease).
