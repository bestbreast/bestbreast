Saggy neck at 35. How can I fix this?
do I need a neck lift?
Apr 17, 2020
Unfortunately liposuction alone or Kybella will not address this degree of skin laxity.  The best results will be obtained with a neck lift combined with a lower facelift.  This procedure will allow excess skin to be removed and muscles tightened to alleviate the laxity both from a superficial and foundational standpoint.  Any modality that does not remove excess tissue and tighten muscles will not obtain satisfactory results.best wishes and good luck
I have 2 scars from giving birth via c section (horizontal & vertical). Can a tummy tuck help?
will a tummy tuck help with both my c section scars
Apr 17, 2020
The short answer is yes.  Most times both of these scars will be easily removed by placing the incision below the lower horizontal incision and removing all skin and fat from this incision to slightly above the belly button.  The umbilicus is then left attached to your abdominal wall and brought out through a new incision.  During the same procedure, separated muscles can also be repaired and additional lipo can be performed if needed for a better contour.Best wishes
