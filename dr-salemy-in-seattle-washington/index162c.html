What liposuction would I need to get a waist?
Liposuction to achieve a nice waistline
Apr 8, 2019
The results of liposuction will depend on a number of factors, including the location and amount of the fat, skin quality and the surgeon's experience.  One important thing to note is that intraabdominal fat (fat behind your muscle around your organs) is often a big contributor to a patient's abdominal contour and this cannot be addressed with liposuction (diet and exercise tends to work best for this area).  Discuss your goals with your surgeon so that you know exactly what you should expect.
