Answer Snippet
Can you exercise to gain muscle mass after a breast fat transfer?
fat transfer
Jul 8, 2020
Once the fat has healed, working out should not be a problem, unless you loose weight. With weight loss, the transferred fat may also loose some volume.
Can I achieve my goals without implants for my tuberous breasts?
breasts
Jul 8, 2020
To achieve the shape you want, you will need implants to help stretch and fill out the lower part of your breasts. You will also need a lift to relocate and improve the shape of your areolae. I would suggest meeting with a board certified plastic surgeon and discussing your options.
Will fat transfer and areola reduction be enough for tuberous breasts?
breasts
Jul 8, 2020
You can have just a fat transfer and an areolar lift, but will get better results with implants and a vertical lift. You won't be able to get your best shape with just fat, nor a full lift with just a areolar lift. I would suggest meeting with a board certified plastic surgeon and discussing your options.
What can I do to make my breasts look better? Wide set, uneven, and bottom heavy
breasts
Jul 8, 2020
You would do well with a lift to the smaller side and a reduction to the larger side, or lift the large side and lift and augmentation with an implant on the smaller side. A fat transfer won't give you what you want.
How does weight gain/weight loss work post-fat transfer?
fat transfer
Jul 8, 2020
With weight gain, the area with the fat transfer can also gin in size. and, the area can loose in size with weight loss.
Do all breast augmentations need drains afterwards?
implants
Jul 8, 2020
I do not use drains for my breast augmentation patients, but some surgeons do. This is based on the surgeon's experience and preference. I also prefer under the muscle for most women. Please discuss your concerns with your surgeon about the use of drains.en.
Two consultations with dramatically different recommendations. How do I choose?
breasts
Jul 8, 2020
The sizes are similar, and all implants have a teardrop shape when standing. The smooth implants typically have less potential problems than the textured. I prefer the subpectoral position for most of my ladies, especially in the thin ladies, to help minimize the edge appearing and give upper breast fullness. I would suggest going with the surgeon who impressed you the most and had the best photos of women similar to you.
I was just wondering if I could get a breast augmentation without a breast lift?
implants
Jul 8, 2020
Yes, you can just choose to have implants, and you will have bigger breasts that sit where they do now. Implants will not provide a lift.
I am 5' tall, 47.5kgs, 30/32 A cup and roughly 82cm around my chest and back. I desire a C/D cup. What
size
Jul 8, 2020
I would suggest that you meet with a board certified plastic surgeon, are measured and examined, and try on a range of sizes that best fits your frame and tissue to see what you like. Your surgeon will help guide you to the best size.
Can areolas always look smaller for e.g. when it's cold and your areola gets smaller and breasts appea
breasts
Jul 8, 2020
If you are happy with your size, then consider a lift and revise the areolae to be smaller. If you want implants, you still may need a lift as well. I would suggest meeting with a board certified plastic surgeon and discussing your concerns and options.
I have a small lump in one breast. It came back fine after a biopsy. Would this be a concern going in
lump
Jul 8, 2020
If the biopsy was clear, you should be ok to proceed with surgery. You might want to ask your surgeon to go ahead and remove this lump so it will no longer be an issue.
How long does it take to recover after breast augmentation?
surgery
Jul 8, 2020
By three weeks you should be able to do your work. If something is very heavy, please ask for help. Your surgeon will be the best one to give you advice since he/she will know how well you are healing.
Does wisdom teeth extraction prior to BA cause problems (such as capsular contracture)?
surgery
Jul 8, 2020
I would suggest discussing this with your surgeon. In my opinion, six weeks with no signs of infection or delayed healing should be adequate. You might also get your dentist opinion if you are healed well enough for an elective procedure.
How is a 24 hour breast augmentation different from a regular BA?
breast
Jul 8, 2020
Any surgery requires time for healing, and you will not be healed at 24 hours. Realistically, you should be back to full activities by 6 weeks. Placing the implants behind the breasts instead of the muscle is less painful and the implants are settled more quickly. However, the healing time is still about the same.
I am an orthodontic assistant. Will a week be enough time off after breast augmentation?
surgery
Jul 8, 2020
A week off should be good as long as you avoid all heavy lifting. You should be able to assist in surgery and the office.
