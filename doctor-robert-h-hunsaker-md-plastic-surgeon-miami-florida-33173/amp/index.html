How common is it to lose breast and nipple sensation after breast implants?
Nipple sensation/function
Sep 5, 2018
In first time breast augmentation of "normal" sizes this is very uncommon - on the order of single digit percentage. Unfortunately, we cannot predict ahead of time who this would happen to. It is quite common to have temporary change/loss of sensation due to the tissue manipulation and swelling.

Implant "size"
Sep 5, 2018
Most patients who require a breast lift wear a bra that "sizes" larger than their actual breast volume. Do I need a breast lift? What type of implants do I need?
Implants
Aug 30, 2018
You do have a short nipple to fold distance which limits the diameter of the implant used. It is advantageous that you only want a small amount of volume increase.. There is really not much, if any, long term cosmetic advantage to using a shaped implant and there are some disadvantages. It will be very important for you to remain open-minded about your options and to have a consultation/examination with one or more board certified surgeons to formulate your plan.
