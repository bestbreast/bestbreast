When changing implants and pocket, what happens to the capsule?
How to fix the muscle when going from under to over
Jul 6, 2020
In most cases, the muscle spasms and distortion are related to detachment of the pectoral muscle, which is done with the dual plane technique. I think it is important to re-attach the muscle, which can be done when converting to above muscle or muscle-splitting biplane (also called split muscle.) In order to make a firm re-attachment, it is safest to preserve a portion of the posterior capsule, which doesn't need to be removed anyway.
1 year post op - bad muscle flex distortion/double bubble? Can anything be done?
Change from dual plane to split muscle for correction of animation/double bubble
Jul 6, 2020
What you are experiencing is unfortunately a common occurrence with dual plane placement, because the muscle is detached and pulls on the scar capsule. This is also a cause of double bubble. The muscle needs to be re-attached behind the implant, in it's normal location. This can be done by converting to the split muscle plane or subfascial. 
Breasts too far apart after augmentation, what are my options?
How chest wall anatomy determines breast implant position
Jul 6, 2020
Your photos show what is called a pectus excavatum, which is a normal variation that is defined by a dip in the sternum (breastbone) between the breasts. The attachment of the muscle is farther apart than average, so the implants have to be farther apart. Fat grafting might be your best option.
What should I do about my silicone implant that shifted/folded?
Need details for recommendation about implant revision
Jul 2, 2020
Since you have been operated on twice, it is especially important to know all of the details in order to offer advice without the benefit of an in-person exam. Photos, implant size and type, and placement (dual plane?) would be helpful to know. You could have capsular contracture, animation deformity, flipped implant, or something else.
Do I need a whole new lift or just revision surgery? I had a breast list with implants in April 2014
Re-do the lift, don't get bigger implants
Jul 2, 2020
What I see in the pictures is a stretched out lower pole of the breast. The "footprint" of the breast is where it should be, you do not need the entire breast moved up. Larger implants will make the problem worse unless you add support with an internal bra using mesh. Really all you need is to re-do the lift, which would remove the pigmented skin.
Is a mini tummy tuck necessary along with lipo?
Lipo vs mini tummy tuck: try to avoid the scar for 22 year old
Jun 30, 2020
The area where a mini tummy tuck would most likely help is covered in your pictures, but in general if you don't have a lot of loose skin it is better to go with liposuction and avoid the scar of a mini tummy tuck. It's worth it when you need it, but my guess is that you probably don't.

Implant Diameter Question?
Implant diameter should not be more than breast diameter
Jun 22, 2020
The measured breast diameter is an external measurement, so if the implants were just as wide the tissue would either be stretched around the implant or squeeze the implant. This can result in rippling, loss of implant support, and other problems.

What caused my double bubble & will it occur again?
Under muscle with split muscle plane should minimize double bubble
Jun 21, 2020
In your case it sounds like you had a combination animation deformity and double bubble, which is why going over the muscle corrected it. Implants under muscle with the dual plane method can cause the double bubble appearance because the cut edge of the muscle adheres to the capsule and pulls on it where you see the groove across the lower breast. If you Have your implants placed under muscle using the split muscle plane you can avoid those problems.
Are implants included in breast reductions?
Consider an internal bra to maintain perky shape with a breast reduction
Jun 21, 2020
Breast reductions all tend to lose upper pole fullness over time. There are 3 options to help maintain the perky shape with upper pole fullness:Internal bra with Galaflex meshfat grafting to the upper poleSome surgeons will do what is called a plus/minus procedure, which is a reduction and implants. To me that seems unnecessarily invasive and counterproductive if the whole purpose of a reduction is to make the breasts smaller.
Breast revision to smaller implants?
Implant profiles and breast lift
Jun 20, 2020
In general, a donut (Benelli) breast lift tends to flatten the breast and higher profile implants counteract that. However, given the size of your current implants and the size you are considering replacing them with, I don't think a donut lift is going to be able to reduce the skin envelope enough anyway. Get the size you want and that will determine the best type of lift. My guess is that a vertical pattern (lollipop) lift will give you the best shape.
