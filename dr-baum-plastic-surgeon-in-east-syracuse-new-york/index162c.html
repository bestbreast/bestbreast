Replacing Ideal Implants with silicone - How long should I ideally wait before having them replaced?
Switching implants
Jul 15, 2019
I’m sorry to hear about your unhappiness with the Ideal implants. I think they are an improvement over traditional saline implants but they are still not as soft and natural feeling as silicone implants. I would recommend waiting at least 3 more months to let your current implants settle into their final spot. This may make it easier when/if you decide to swap out your implants. I would continue to follow up with your surgeon so that he/she can continue to give you guidance. Best wishes.

Will Natrelle full profile, 485cc, over the muscle, look unnatural?
Jul 14, 2019
Based on your photos, I think you need a revision of your breast lift and perhaps slightly larger implants to give you the upper pole fullness you are looking for. Switching the implants to on top of the muscle will likely cause significant bottoming out, less upper pole fullness with time, and an increased risk of recurrent capsular contracture. Best regards.

Would implants alone give me a full breast appearance or make them look more saggy? Do I need a lift?
Implants vs. implants with lift
Mar 29, 2019
Based on your photo, you would most benefit from implants with a lift. You could just do a breast augmentation alone but your breasts will be droopy and not be centered on the implants. After having a tummy tuck with just the horizontal incision, can I go back and have just a vertical incision?
Mar 17, 2019
This is a very interesting question. In order to get the benefit from adding the vertical incision, you will need to have the transverse incision re-opened to facilitate removal of tissue and restore your waistline. Unfortunately, there is no way to remove this extra tissue without opening up the transverse incision. I would speak to your plastic surgeon about the recovery, costs, and scarring before proceeding with the surgery. Best wishes.
