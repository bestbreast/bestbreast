Can capsular contracture be corrected with revision or will it just return?
Capsular contracture
Oct 30, 2019
Capsular contracture is very frustrating in that once you have it, even after the capsule and implant are removed and a new implant placed, you are at higher risk of recurrence than others. I recommend a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to confirm your diagnosis of capsular contracture and to discuss your options going forward.

Is is possible to have surgery without steri strips or glue?
Dressings After Surgery
Oct 24, 2019
It is absolutely possible to avoid Steri Strips, surgical glue, and adhesive with a breast lift. Dressings vary between surgeons, but in a patient with sensitive skin and previous reactions to these dressings, a dry dressing or antibiotic ointment and a non-stick gauze are suitable alternatives. Make sure to mention this to your plastic surgeon prior to your surgery to ensure they are aware of your previous reactions.
