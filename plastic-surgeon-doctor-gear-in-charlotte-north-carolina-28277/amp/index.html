How is a 24 hour breast augmentation different from a regular BA?
So-called 24 hr breast augmentation
Jul 8, 2020
Its just an advertising gimmick.  Surgery is surgery and there is no magic that can bypass the physiology of surgery and surgical recovery.  The concepts behind the 24 hr recovery was popularized by a well known breast surgeon in Texas.  There is validity to some of the concepts involved, and there are indeed ways to make the recovery less toxic including careful, atraumatic, bloodless dissection, use of long acting local anesthetics.
