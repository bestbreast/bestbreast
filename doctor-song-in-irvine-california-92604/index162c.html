Is it still possible to breastfeed after a BA?
Breast feeding after BA
Jul 30, 2016
You should absolutely be able to breast feed after BA surgery as long as a couple smart decisions are made. First, use an IMF or under the breast incision. This will avoid damage to the gland and duct system of the breast. Second, do not select an implant that is too large. Large implants will create more atrophy and long-term damage to your breast. If you select a medium sized implant and use an IMF incision, you will be protecting your breasts, and likely achieve the best looking result!...
