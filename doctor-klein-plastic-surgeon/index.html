Surgery wtihout steri-strips or glue

You absolutely can do any surgery without steri-strips or glue. While we often use them, they are not essential, so if you suspect that you are sensitive, ask your surgeon to avoid using them. Scar creams may be helpful, and I sometimes use simple paper tape without glue on scars. You can always test paper tape at another site.
