Limited incision tummy tuck worth it?
Mini tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
Removal of skin excess along the lower abdomen and pelvis with a low pelvic scar is a reasonable plan, in your case.
Does a substantial diastasis recti and an umbilical hernia make it more difficult to repair
Rectus diastasis and tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
An umbilical hernia and rectus diastasis can be repaired during a tummy tuck procedure to flatten the abdominal wall.
Will insurance pay for my abdominoplasty because of my diastasis recti?
Rectus diastasis and insurance payments
Mar 17, 2020
The rectus diastasis is a separation of the vertically oriented rectus muscles, which created a rounded or 'bulging' abdominal contour. The condition is not a hernia or abodminal wall defect, which would require reconstruction. It is merely a cosmetic issue - thusly, tummy tuck surgery with rectus muscle repair is not covered by insurance companies.
How can I tell I have enough skin elasticity to have a low tummy tuck scar?
Tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
You appear to be a great candidate for a standard tummy tuck with a lower pelvic scar and reshaping of the belly button. We use the opportunity to tighten the abdominal muscles when performing tummy tuck surgery. Liposuction of the waist and hips can be done for patients that need additional contouring.
Do I need a fleur de lis tummy tuck?
Game time decision for FDL Tummy Tuck
Mar 17, 2020
I would consider doing a tummy tuck with the option of making an intra-operative decision on how much skin laxity you had in the vertical domain after setting your skin tension with the horizontal wound closure. I've done that a few times and I've been pleased with the result.
Follow-up question: Do I need a tummy tuck to achieve a flat tummy, reduce love handles and
Liposuction considerations
Mar 17, 2020
For a young female patient like yourself with no past pregnancies and zero skin laxity - the answer is liposuction of the abdomen, waist and hips, every time.
What's a good hemoglobin level for an anemic patient to be at prior to an Abdominoplasty TT?
Hematocrit and pre-op tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
Assuming your anemia has been worked up by your PCP and you are on appropriate supplementation - its not unusual for some women to have mild anemia in the peri-operative period. I certainly would feel comfortable doing a tummy tuck for a female patient with a hematocrit of 33 on iron supplementation.
What can I do about my stomach and body after weight loss?
Tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
Once you have arrived at a body weight that you are comfortable with, schedule a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss a tummy tuck and liposuction of the waist and hips.
Which techniques can be used to fix suspected internal nasal valve collapse?
Internal nasal valve collapse treatment
Mar 17, 2020
The classic treatment for internal nasal valnce airflow obstruction is spreader flaps or spreader grafts to create more space between the nasal septum and the upper lateral nasal cartilages. This can be done during a rhinoplasty procedure.
Will I have to have a tummy tuck (I like my belly button) if I get a BBL and smaller waist?
BBL and tummy tuck considerations
Mar 17, 2020
A reasonable plan would be liposuction of the upper and lower abdomen, waist and hips along with the thighs and use that fat for autologous fat transfer to the buttock. A tummy tuck might not be necessary in your case.
Do you think PSs will be motivated to give discounts on surgery considering the coronavirus
Elective plastic surgery in the time of Covid-19
Mar 17, 2020
Plastic Surgeons are experiencing a slow-down in new patient consultations. The surgery centers in which they operate have also slowed down or completely stopped doing elective surgical procedures. For this reason, our practice continues to see new patient consultations, with a goal of scheduling elective cosmetic surgery over the next few months when our surgery center permits us to operate.
Will cutting the incision smaller make your boobs higher?
The incision for breast augmentation
Oct 23, 2019
The incision and its length and position are important - but the incision does not determine the position of your implants, how rapidly they settle and the integrity of your infra-mammary fold. These three issues are critical for breast augmentation results.
What kind of breast augmentation or reconstruction is suitable for me?
Breast implant considerations
Oct 23, 2019
Your photos are not standardized, so its difficult to say for certain - I would bet that you could get away with a dual-plane breast augmentation without a breast lift and have a nice cosmetic result. See a board certified PLASTIC surgeon in your area for a consultation.
What are the risks of gaining belly fat post circumferential body lift?
Weight changes after lower body lift surgery
Oct 23, 2019
Weight loss after a lower body lift procedure will likely enhance your post-operative results - the loss of intra-abdominal fat always improves contour. The opposite is also true. Good luck !
If I go home the same day as my surgery how are these needs monitored?
After care considerations post-op body contouring
Oct 23, 2019
For patients in your age group with potential medical co-morbidities (high blood pressure, cardiac history, CPAP use etc), we recommend staying the night at the ambulatory surgery center. Not all surgery centers off the overnight stay option - find a board certified plastic surgeon that can offer this to you.
