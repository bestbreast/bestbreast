Do I need a lift?
Lift
Feb 28, 2015
Hi, there is no right or wrong answer here. You could possibly get away without a lift depending on the implants you choose. I would always go below the muscle. A implant with a lift is another option but you would have to accept permanent scarring on the breasts. I would advise to do implant allow and re evaluate in 6 weeks. You can always do a lift later. Good luck!
Which Breast Implant Placement Gives a More Natural Appearance?
Natural appearance
Feb 27, 2015
Hi, silicone implants placed below the muscle tend to give the most natural looking results. Also, the anatomical implants also help to give a natural look. Ask you doctor about both. Good luck!

Saline vs silicone
Feb 27, 2015
Hi, by far silicone is a better choice. The newer implants last longer and have much lower rates of leak or capsular contracture. They also look so much more natural and will most likely last longer
Should Breast Implants Be Placed Under or over the Muscle?
Above or below the muscle
Feb 24, 2015
In my opinion the implant should always go behind the muscle. its better for so many reasons. Better looking, feeling, stay in positions longer, better for mammography and breast feeding, less capsular contracture. To name a few.
Will Pregnancy Ruin my Breast Implants?
Pregnancy
Feb 23, 2015
Hi, pregnancy can have different effects on the breasts The implants should not be affected at all but the breast tissue on top of the implants can be affected. They can enlarge and hang more after pregnancy and breast feeding, but the implants should be fine.
Do Breast Enlargement Creams Work?

I have had breast implants for 14 years. How Long Do Breast Implants Last?

Hi, implants vary in how long they last. I usually tell patients that saline last around 10 years and silicone, especially the form stable one and can last 15-20 years.
