How long is the usual time to take off work after a mommy makeover?
Time off work
Apr 9, 2020
The postop restrictions are based on the procedure you are having; for most procedures we like patients up and walking soon after surgery and patients can drive as soon as they are off narcotics. For more extensive procedures we recommend no heavy lifting or exercise for up to 6 weeks after surgery. It is best to review these with your surgeon

Best to find a plastic surgeon who you are comfortable with and discuss your goals during the consultation. Good luck!
Would I be able to have a subglandular augmentation?
Subglandular breast augmentation
Apr 9, 2020
Thank you for sending your question and picture, you do appear to have enough breast tissue to "hide" an implant placed above the muscle. Keep in mind that your chances of implant visibility and/or capsular contracture may be increased with this approach but you also will avoid the "animation deformity" caused by placing the implant under the muscle. Best to discuss in detail with your plastic surgeon, good luck!
