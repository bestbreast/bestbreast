Will Botox help me to achieve brow symmetry?
Will Botox help me to achieve brow symmetry?
Jun 24, 2020
Yes, in the majority of cases, Botox can be successfully used to achieve brow symmetry.  Once you have been examined, your doctor will be able to determine how much Botox will be required to achieve the better symmetry.  It may take one or two rounds of injections to determine the best dosage in your particular case.  The injection results will last about 3 months and will need to be repeated to maintain the affect.  Best wishes, Dr. Lepore.
Are nipple piercing rejections a sign that my body will reject breast implants?
Nipple piercing rejection and breast implants?
Jun 24, 2020
The nipple contains many ducts in which bacteria can concentrate, and piercing this area can and does result in a higher infection rate when piercings are performed.  However, breast augmentation surgery incisions, would avoid this area of higher infection risk.  I don't think you need to be concerned that having had a problem with your nipple piercing would result in a higher risk of breast implant infection/rejection.  Best wishes, Dr. Lepore.
Would you recommend a tummy tuck considering the current state of my belly?
Would you recommend a tummy tuck?
Jun 24, 2020
Given that you have lost 50 lbs. and already regularly work out, I believe you would be a very good candidate for a tummy tuck.  Abdominal exercises can improve abdominal muscle tone, but it can't do anything about the loose skin.  Exercise is still important, and plastic surgery is not a substitute for this, but the surgery should help where the exercise cannot.  Seek out a board certified plastic surgeon with extensive tummy tuck experience.  Best wishes, Dr. Lepore.
