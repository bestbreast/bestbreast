I am no doctor but I think I have an infection?
Wound healing delay versus infection
Jan 14, 2017
The photos you show are very consistent with delayed wound healing, which is quite common at the lower T junction of the breast with this type of surgery. It can closely resemble infection, but is a result of poor blood supply to the bottom of the breast and the challenge of healing two incisions that come to a point. However, I would closely follow up with your plastic surgeon to make sure you do not also have an infection. Best of luck.
