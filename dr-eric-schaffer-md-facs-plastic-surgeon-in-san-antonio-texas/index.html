I am 5’7” and 165 pounds. Opinions on 650 cc?
Opinion on 650 cc implants
Oct 29, 2019
Thank you for your question regarding 650 cc implants.  To obtain a pleasing result you will most probably need a lift with the implants.  When the nipple/areolar area is below the natural breast crease then a breast lift will reposition the nipple in the center of the breast.  The implant will then give you volume at the top of the breast which I would place under the muscle.  Good luck.
Fibrocystic breast, breast cancer family history and implants?
Family history of breast cancer and Breast Augmentation
Aug 26, 2019
Thank you for a great question!  There are many patients like you that have fibrocystic breasts but you are still a candidate for Breast Augmentation surgery.  The technique I use is placing the implant behind the muscle through an armpit incision which means the implants are never in contact with breast tissue.  Prior to surgery, we will have you obtain a current mammogram to make sure you are a safe, healthy patient.  Good luck.

Can I remove flabby arm skin and can it be transferred to my butt?​
Can you remove flabby arm skin and transfer to buttocks?
Jul 1, 2019
Thank you for your question.  Flabby arm skin can be removed but this is not used to enhance the buttocks.  Adipose tissue (fat) is what is needed to contour the buttocks which is usually obtained by liposuction of the abdomen or back.  You need a large volume of fat to transfer to make a visible difference.  Brachioplasty can be performed to give you non-flabby arms.  Good luck.
DVT ten years ago during my first pregnancy in ‘09. No complications since. Can I safely proceed with
History of DVT, possibility of having a tummy tuck
Jul 1, 2019
Patients that are at risk for DVT can still have a tummy tuck with the proper clearance.  I usually have the patient visit with their hematologist for a work up and advice to help protect you during your recovery.  We routinely use Lovenox  after surgery and have our patients use Compression leg devices at home for 2 weeks after surgery.  Discuss your concerns with your plastic surgeon and good luck.
