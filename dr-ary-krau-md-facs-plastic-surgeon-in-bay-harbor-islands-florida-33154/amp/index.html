Can I just get a breast lift with no implants?
breast lift with imkplants
Dec 26, 2018
It is not possible to achieve long lasting upper pole fullness without the use of implants. To convert large droopy breasts to a more perky look, one needs to remove that part of the breast that sags below the crease and convert them to smaller perky breasts.  Behind these newly formed perky breasts an implant can be placed of essentially any size.  Simply put, large droopy breasts are converted to smaller perky breasts where a simple implant augmentation is performed.Good luck,Ary Krau MD
Do I need different size implants to achieve symmetrical breasts?
lift with implants

Generally speaking, when undergoing breast lift with implants, the larger breast is reduced and lifted more so as to achieve better symmetry.  Once this is done same  sized implants should be used.  The exception is when chest wall asymmetry exists.Good luck,Ary Krau MD

aug vs lift
Feb 11, 2018
There are essentially 3 groups of breasts.  Those that absolutely do not need a lift.  those that absolutely need a lift, and those, as in your case, can undergo either procedure.  In your case I believe you will get quite an acceptable result whether you have a lift or not.  With a lift, however, your breast will be higher but at the cost of more scarring.  You'll need too weigh these pros and cons.Good luck, Ary Krau MD FACSA
Why are my implants sticking out and changing shape? 3 years post silicone moderate + 495 smooth imp
ripples
Feb 11, 2018
It is unlikely that your implants are ruptured. you have visible ripples or "knuckles" of your implants.  This is seen most often with either saline or textured implants, and when implants are above the muscle.  Sometimes capsular contracture can cause this but I doubt that is the case with you as you show a similar knuckle on your left where the breast looks looser.
