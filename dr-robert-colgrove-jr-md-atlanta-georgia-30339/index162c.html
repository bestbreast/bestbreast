Will 600 cc be too big for me?
Will 600 cc be too big for me?
Jul 25, 2019
To determine if a particular size breast implant is appropriate for my patients, I like to measure the breast width diameter and evaluate with Vectra 3D analysis. Based on breast width diameter and the ability of the skin and muscle to stretch will determine if this implant will fit. Sometimes the final determination is made in the operating room so be prepared to go smaller.
2-months Post-Op BA; Double Bubble Forming?
Double Bubble Trouble
Apr 18, 2013
Unfortunately, it does appear that you may be developing a "double bubble". You may try taping the area to see if it may flatten down. Otherwise, you may need surgery in the future to correct this. A breast lift may be indicated as well to achieve optimal results. You should discuss this with your surgeon to see what he/she suggests. I personally do not like performing revisional surgery on my patients until 6 months post op.
