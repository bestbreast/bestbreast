I'm still trying to decide on just a lift or lift with saline implant. I'm about a 34b and usu
Breast Lift alone or with Implants
Oct 12, 2015
Generally a breast lift alone, unless there is a significant amount of glandular tissue, will actually diminish slightly the size of the breast. This is most obvious when there is a loose skin envelope. Breast lifts alone will also not create much in the way of upper pole projection. That is why the majority of true breast lifts require an implant, otherwise you will still require a padded bra.

How severe are my tuberous breasts?
