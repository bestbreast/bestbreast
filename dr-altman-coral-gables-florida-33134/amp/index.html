2 weeks post-op, will I need a revision rhinoplasty?
Healing After Rhinoplasty
Aug 22, 2018
Your concerns are normal for this stage in the healing process.  I can see the fullness you are describing in your pictures, but it is impossible to tell if this is simply swelling or residual bone in the area.  Give yourself the appropriate amount of time to heal - at least 3 - 6 months.  If the area does not improve, it is likely your surgeon will be able to correct this with a minor revision.  
Did I go too large? 5’3, 127 pounds (375cc) 10 days post op.
Did I Go Too Big?
Aug 22, 2018
