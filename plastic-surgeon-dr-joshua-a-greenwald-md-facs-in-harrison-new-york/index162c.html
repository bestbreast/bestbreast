After a weight loss (95 lbs), my breasts only downsized by one cup. Why?
Breast size
Oct 3, 2019
Weight loss may result in reduction of breast size.  Breast are composed of fat and breast tissue.  Some women have very fatty, less dense breasts (will decrease in size with weight loss) while other women have very dense breasts with little fat (minimal change with weight loss).Dr. Greenwald
