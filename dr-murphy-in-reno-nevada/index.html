Can I have a larger high profile implant without a lift?
Augmentation without a mastopexy
Jan 4, 2017
An examination would be needed as in the pictures it is difficult to see how much asymmetry and ptosis you have. You most likely could have a breast augmentation without a lift however you would need to go with a larger implant to fill the skin you have. You may consider doing a Benelli or Circumareolar Mastopexy with implants instead of a full lift.  All the best to you.

Breast Implant Size
