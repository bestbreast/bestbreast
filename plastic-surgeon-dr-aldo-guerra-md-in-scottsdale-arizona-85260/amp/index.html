I’m 7 weeks post op muscle repair and tummy tuck. Is it normal to have swelling?
Is it normal to have swelling?
Feb 19, 2019
Yes. Swelling after tummy tuck is completely normal. I suggest lymphatic massage, Arnica, and Bromelain to all my patients to speed the process. Having a professional massage is a great way to eradicate swelling. After 7 weeks, you're only about 13% of the time healing. With major body contouring surgery, like tummy tuck, it takes twelve months for the final result and for all the swelling to go away. Best wishes and congratulations,Dr. ALDO
