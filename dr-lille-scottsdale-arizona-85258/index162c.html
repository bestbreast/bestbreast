Is it necessary to split procedures into 2 surgeries?
Splitting up surgery
May 20, 2019
The  benefits of Splitting up the surgery  are several fold:1) decrease health problems like reducing the risks of a deep vein clot2) ability the remove more skin fromthe breasts during a breast lift3 achieve a greater lift to the breasts due to enhanced skin removal4) and finally the ability the use a bigger breast implant without the concern of breast skin incisional separation or death. You’ll have a great experience!

Can facelift swelling recur after it's healed?
Facelift swelling
Apr 15, 2019
It’s not unusual to have swelling following a facelift up to 6 to 9 months. Swelling often occurs in cycles after the initial three month period. This commonly occurs following physical activities.Stabilization  usually occurs after nine months to a year subsequent from surgery. You are still very early in the postoperative recovery phase but seeing your plastic surgeon for assurance will be a great idea.

What’s the max my BMI can be to get a tummy tuck or is there a doctor that does tummy tucks on women
BMI and tummy tucks
Oct 14, 2018
Great question. Studies have shown that a BMI 35 and over has increased precipitously the risks of doing surgery in a tummy tuck.  Those risks include infection, wound break down, deep vein clot's and others.  I prefer to perform a tummy tuck in patients who have a BMI that is equal to or less than 30. In addition to reducing the risks, having a thinner abdominal skin flap allows for more skin resection, greater skin flap mobility and enhanced  contouring. Good luck!
