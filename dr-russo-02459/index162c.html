Do facelifts and neck lifts turn out worse when the patient has had RF microneedling previously?
facelift
Feb 20, 2020
Anything that causes injury and subsequent inflammation in the skin can cause scar tissue to form. This definitely makes the surgery more difficult, but surgeons are used to working under these circumstances, and it should not affect your result
Can weight loss of only 10 pounds reduce volume in face?
facelift
Feb 20, 2020
In general, once a woman loses fat from her face, it is difficult to gain it back despite weight gain. It is not uncommon to have filler placed to regain that youthful look
Is there a variation in facelift technique that can restore volume to cheeks? Or are fat/fillers ne
facelift
Feb 20, 2020
It really varies from person to person. In general most patients undergoing a facelift have lost volume from their faces and do benefit from fillers or fat in the cheek area. I would do the facelift and reevaluate after several months. If you need more volume it is easy to do at that point
How do I achieve a smoother look under my eyes, and a less buggy-eyed look all together?
fillers
Feb 20, 2020
I would try a little botox to treat the hypertrophy of the muscle under your eye along with filler placed on the bone to add volume to the tear trough area
Is it possible to have fullness restored without a lift and without looking augmented?
breast augmentation
Feb 20, 2020
Due to your preexisting breast ptosis, I think you will need a mastopexy to get the best result. I think you will do well with an implant (submuscular) plus a lift. The degree of lift will depend upon size and shape of the chosen implant
Do I require a lift along with the 400cc high profile implant I'm hoping to go with?
breast augmentation
Feb 20, 2020
From the photos alone, it is difficult to say if an implant alone will be enough. It also depends upon the shape and size of the implant. Once you have been examined and picked an implant it will be easier to predict if you need a lift. I would not be surprises if you ended up with a lift of some sort
Is a breast augmentation safe if I have RA and Lupus (SLE), and am on Humira and Plaquenil?
breast augmentation

There are a lot of concerns with this combination of illnesses and medications. I would have a talk with your doctors about the risks of surgery in this situation

Is it safe to have Botox in the neck with health conditions?
botox
Jan 16, 2020
It is safe to have Botox even with a thyroid condition. There is no interaction with the thyroid gland as the botox is injected into the muscle which is superficial to the thyroid

Jan 16, 2020
Yes, this can happen. You may have gotten a little to much botox in your forehead which can result in a drooping of the brow. I would tell your doc and try using a lower dose in the forehead
