For lower eyelid blepharoplasty, should fat/skin be removed or just rearranged?
Lower Eyelid Surgery
Sep 12, 2018
It is important to be very conservative with skin removal for lower eyelid surgery.  The risk is that removing too much skin will lead to a change in shape or positioning of the lower eyelid margin.  In most people, removing or rearranging the fat and then performing laser or chemical peel on the skin can give a great result.  For those with excess skin, then a skin pinch or conservative skin excision may be considered.
