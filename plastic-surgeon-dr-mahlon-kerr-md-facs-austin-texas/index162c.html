Combo surgery

Would a mommy makeover be the best option for me?
Mommy makeover
Jun 18, 2019

I have a hernia and a diastasis recti. Would they fix the diastasis recti?
Diastasis
Jun 4, 2019
In my experience they may cover a small portion for a true hernia repair but diastasis alone is typically not covered. I think you’ll be surprised to see how little the coverage for the hernia would be.  This is the reason why most busy plastic surgeons don’t accept any insurance plans. Good luck!
