How long should you wait between having a TT and breast lift with implants and having a BBL done?
Tummy tuck, BBL, breast surgery
May 21, 2018
In most cases all three procedures could be done at once. Typically when a tummy tuck is done, Lipo suction is also used to remove the unwanted fat. This is the perfect time in most cases to perform the BBL and put the unwanted fat to good use. I would recommend consulting with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss what the best option would be for you. Good luck!
