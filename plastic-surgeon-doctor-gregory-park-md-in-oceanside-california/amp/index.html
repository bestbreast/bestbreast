Can this animation deformity be fixed?
Can this animation deformity be fixed?
Dec 19, 2019
Animation deformity can be eliminated by placement of breast implants above the muscles. The flexion deformity can also be reduced by converting your current submuscular pockets to Multiplane-Split Muscle pockets. Standard submuscular placement of breast implants will always have animation deformities regardless of implant size. Best wishes.
