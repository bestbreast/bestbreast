Can getting labia removed that is extremely close to your clitoris/clitoral hood cause nerve damage/l
Labiaplasty is Unlikely to cause Nerve Damage to Clitoris
May 18, 2020
This is not a silly question.  In fact, it is a common one and an important one to ask your surgeon.  As with any surgery, there are risks of complications like decreased sensation, but in the right hands and with excellent knowledge of the anatomy, the risk is quite low.  The excess skin of the labia near the clitoral hood is common.  Make sure the surgeon you go with has vast experience with happy patients after labiaplasty.

Would I be able to get a tummy tuck even with my previous c-sections?
Tummy Tuck after C-Sections
Apr 21, 2020
Tummy tuck is a wonderful surgery after having children and even better after C-section.  Having prior C-sections does not negatively effect your tummy tuck.  In fact, usually the tummy tuck scar is placed below the old C-section scars and is removed with the excess skin of the tummy.  Of course, you should see a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon who specializes in body contouring to have an in depth consultation to discuss your options.

Will a breast lift help an underboob roll?
Underboob with Breast Lift - Liposuction at the same time may Help
Apr 15, 2020
Thank you for your inquiry.  Without seeing you in person or reviewing your photos and doing an online or virtual consult, it would be hard to tell you definitively whether the breast lift would help that area.  Typically, the breast lift improves the excess skin and sagging above the breast crease (inframammary fold).  At times, liposuction is done to the fat roll under the IMF to reduce the appearance of fat on top of the ribs.

Why does the treatment zone exclude the nasolabial fold area?
Ulthera over nasolabial fold
Jan 9, 2020
It is recommended to avoid treatment with the deeper transducers over the nasolabial fold because of a small risk of causing temporary injury to a nerve located in this area.  However, by treating the surrounding tissues, you are still able to safely achieve a lifting of this tissue.  Ulthera is an excellent treatment for stimulating collagen and achieving lift in the face for patients with early skin laxity.

Is it safe to have Lumecca IPL done if you have a permanent retainer or braces?
IPL with Braces
Jan 9, 2020
IPL is fantastic for rejuvenating skin and targeting pigmentation and veins of the face.  IPL treatments are safe with braces or a retainer.  Occasionally, patients may feel tingling or increased sensation over these devices during the treatment.  If this happens,  placing a cotton roll in the mouth will reduce these sensations.  

Do the antibodies to cow's milk protein (allergy) render Dysport ineffective?
Allergy to Cow's Milk Protein
Jan 9, 2020
Patients with an allergy to cow's milk protein should not receive Dysport.  The risk isn't of an ineffective treatment but rather an allergic reaction.  Patients who are lactose intolerant are able to safely receive Dysport.

Some say that Ultherapy can be used around the eyes, others say it should never be used around the ey
Ulthera around the eyes
Dec 9, 2019
Ulthera can safely be used on the lower lids, crows feet and brow.  It is not used on the upper eyelids.  Ulthera is a fantastic non-surgical procedure to help elevate the brow and soften crepiness of the lower lids.  The procedure is used routinely and safely in all of these areas.  The device has more superficial transducers that safely target these areas of the face.

Are the results of Morpheus8 permanent or do the treatments need to be continued yearly?
Maintaining Results
Dec 9, 2019
Morpheus8 is a fantastic minimally invasive treatment for facial rejuvenation.  Our patients start with a series of 3 treatments for optimal results.  With any treatment for facial rejuvenation, patients still continue to experience the signs of aging, so maintenance is required.  If a patient is practicing sun precautions and following a good at home regimen, less maintenance will be needed.  Most patients can expect to retreat every 12-24 months.

Is the Morpheus8 treatment safe to use after fillers?
Morpheus and Fillers
Dec 9, 2019
It is safe and common  to have Morpheus after a filler treatment.  I recommend at least 2 weeks for any bruising or swelling to resolve.  Our patients see fantastic results with a combination of fillers and Morpheus8 for facial rejuvenation.

I’m a small young woman - about size 0. Is CoolSculpting still effective? 5'7 and 108 lbs
CoolSculpting small pockets of fat
Dec 9, 2019
It would be best to see an experienced provider who can determine if your trouble areas are candidates for treatment. In our practice, we often treat patients at their goal weight who have stubborn areas of fat.  The fat must be "pinchable" enough to fit in to the CoolSculpting applicator.  If there is not enough tissue to achieve proper suction with the applicator, the treatment will be ineffective.

Is there any contraindication to having Emsculpt post muscle repair and hernia repair?
EmSculpt after hernia repair
Nov 5, 2019
Your hernia repair and tummy tuck should be completely healed five years after your surgery.  As long as you do not have any metal in your abdomen (or elsewhere), you can receive EmSculpt treatments.  However, it is always a good practice to discuss any surgeries with your provider prior to treatment.

Nov 5, 2019
