When having a tummy tuck, what exactly are you tested for in the LABS prior to surgery?
Lab tests before a tummy tuck
May 29, 2019
The testing you need both laboratory and otherwise before a procedure is determined largely by your age and your underlying medical conditions. For example, a healthy person under the age of 50 with no major medical problems may not need any testing. Also, women of child bearing age require testing to prove that they're not pregnant. In the end, it's up to your doctor to determine what testing is required.
