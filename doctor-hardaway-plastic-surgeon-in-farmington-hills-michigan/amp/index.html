Is BBL an option for me or is my body a lost cause? What's my best treatment plan?
BBL candidates
Jan 12, 2019
Liposuction with a BBL along with a tummy tuck will give you good results.  Contouring of your back including the flanks will help give you the contour that you are looking for.  If you do not want a tummy tuck at this time, it can be performed at a later date if there is too much loose skin on your abdomen after liposuction.  It is important to have realistic expectations to prevent post operative disappointment.
I want a BBL but I have a hard time swallowing pills/pain meds, are there any alternatives to pil
Post operative pain medication
Jan 12, 2019
Since you have difficulty swallowing pills, there are some pain medications that come in a liquid form.  Discuss this with your board certified plastic surgeon who will suggest which liquid pain medications will be best for you.
Should I get an umbilical hernia repair or abdominoplasty?
Umbilical Hernia Repair with a Tummy Tuck
Oct 14, 2018
An umbilical hernia can be repaired at the same surgical setting as an abdominoplasty.  The repair of the diastasis recti will help strengthen the muscle, especially if the diastasis is very large.
What is my best procedure if I am at a 36 BMI and my main goal is to relieve hip pain?
BMI of 36 and HIp Pain
Oct 14, 2018
Unfortunately a tummy tuck is not a form of weight reduction.  If your orthopedic surgeon feels that removing  overhanging abdominal skin would help relieve some discomfort, then a panniculectomy may help.  You may have to pursue other surgical means of weight reduction in order to have a significant affect.   
Should I consider a TT revision?
Tummy Tuck Revision
Sep 19, 2018
Excision of the lower skin will give improvement based on the pictures that you have provided.  Sometimes after a tummy tuck, the lower skin will relax again especially if they was a significant amount of stretch marks present.  Consult your plastic surgeon for a complete evaluation.
I've consulted with two surgeons and one wants to use a drains as the other does not. What is the
Drains and Breast Implant Revision
Jul 27, 2018
Whether or not drains are used in implant revision surgery is based on surgeon's preference.  There is no exact right or wrong answer.  Drains are often used with removal of a capsule as a precaution to prevent any blood or fluid build up around the implant.   
Is it possible to get a Brazilian butt lift when you have COPD and heart disease?
Health Problems and Cosmetic Surgery
Jul 21, 2018
Prior to cosmetic surgery as with any surgery, medical stability is a must.  Since cosmetic surgery is elective, if there is any significant risk, the surgery may not be advisable.  Medical clearance from your internist, pulmonologist and cardiologist is advisable. Take their advise on whether you should have any surgical procedures..  
Is it safe to do a tummy tuck if my iron level is 11?
Blood work prior to a Tummy Tuck
Jul 21, 2018
I am assuming that you are referring to your Hemoglobin level.  The normal Hemoglobin is usually between 12 and 15.  You are slightly below the normal level.  Have an evaluation by your medical physician to assess the cause of your Hemoglobin.  If the tummy tuck is being performed alone with no additional procedures you should be fine.  Get clearance from you medical physician regarding your blood work prior to surgery.

How successful is the use of slow-release lidocaine with a tummy tuck/lipo procedure?
Exparel to decrease post op discomfort after a Tummy Tuck
Jul 21, 2018
Exparel does decrease post op discomfort after a tummy tuck.  The oral pain medication is usually sufficient after the Exparel slowly wears off.  In regards to liposuction, most patients tolerate it well although everyone experiences discomfort differently.
Is an implant exchange as painful as the first surgery?
Implant Exchange and Post Op Discomfort
Jul 21, 2018
Post op discomfort is usually less after the second surgery because the muscle and tissue has already been stretched from the first surgical procedure.  You stated that you are getting larger implants so there will be some discomfort.  Discuss your concerns with your plastic surgeon.
Am I a good candidate for the BBL procedure? Do I have to get the TT?
Tummy, Liposuction and BBL
Mar 30, 2018
Based on your photos, a tummy tuck with liposuction of the back with fat transfer to the buttocks would give you the best results. Liposuction of the abdomen does not tighten the abdominal wall muscles or removed excess skin. I would suggest that you be near your goal weight prior to surgery for optimal postoperative results.
How soon after a consult, can you schedule the actual procedure?
Time Between Consultation and Surgery
Mar 30, 2018
The wait for surgery from the time of consultation varies with the surgeon's schedule. There is basic lab work that is needed prior to surgery and other test depending on your medical history. If you are considering surgery soon, I would suggest that you schedule a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon at your earliest convenience.
Could I get quotes and book an appointment before losing weight?
Surgical Quotes before Weight Loss
Mar 30, 2018
Congratulations on your weight loss goal. An estimate for surgery can be given but I usually suggest that you are closer to your weight goal for optimal surgical results. Make an appointment with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss your goals and desires.
Can I change my implants from subglandular to submuscular?
Changing the Position of Breast Implants
Mar 28, 2018
Your breast implants can be changed from a subglandular to submuscular position. The capsule would be removed at the time of surgery. You should get a second opinion from a board certified plastic surgeon in your area and discuss your concerns at the time of the consultation.
