Will my breast implants look bigger once they drop?
Nope
Aug 24, 2018
The breasts will not look bigger as they drop but they will change shape and although you see size as the most important part of the process, plastic surgeons also look at the shape. You may find that you like the shape once they’ve settled.
Implants Don’t Seem Big Enough! Will They Eventually Get Bigger?
Size
Aug 24, 2018
The implant don’t get bigger over time BUT you are only looking at the size while plastic surgeons also look at the shape. The shape will improve and you may find that you are really happy in the long run.
Breast Implant Size and Placement for Slim, Sporty Woman?
2 things
Aug 24, 2018
It is impossible to tell what will work without an exam. Also, 25 cc will not make a bit of difference. You need an exam to give an opinion. Good luck!
Will 200cc Breast Implants Give a B Cup?
It always depends
Aug 24, 2018
Think about this logically. If you are now a C cup, will 200 ccs make you a B cup? Obviously not. Also consider that cup size can never be guaranteed because of 2 reasons: 1. the bra manufacturers decided on cup size and no 2 manufacturers agree so cup size is completely invented. 2. some women like to “swim” in there bras while others like a tight fitting bra.
Size Help! Debating Between 175cc and 200cc
Impossible
Aug 24, 2018
It is absolutely impossible for you to tell whether a certain sized implant will fit your body based on a picture. I suggest finding some photos of women you find attractive and letting your plastic surgeon decide on a size. 
Too Small Breast Augmentation?
Plenty of time
Aug 24, 2018
6 months is enough time for you to heal. It appears the surgeon did a beautiful job but it is possible you’ve decided you like a bigger look. I suggest using an implant with a similar width but a higher profile. 
What Size Implants for Small, Natural-looking Breasts?
photos
Aug 24, 2018
I’ve found that the best way to figure our what size will give you the best look is to bring in photos of what you think is attractive. I constantly get patients telling me they want to look natural but show photos of very round unnatural breasts. An experienced surgeon will be able to find the perfect implant for you.
6 days post op. UNHAPPY with small implants.
Too early
Aug 24, 2018
It is not uncommon for patients to be concerned right after surgery. 6 days is too short of a time to know what the result will be. I suggest waiting 3 months before deciding if you like the result. Anybody in this forum that tells you to switch your implants after only 6 days does not do enough of this kind of surgery. 
Breast Augmentation Recovery: What Can I Expect?
recovery
Aug 24, 2018
You will have pain for up to a week, swelling for 2 weeks, and your breasts will settle over a 6 month period (usually by 6 weeks). If you have a desk job, you can go back to work within 48 hours.
I would like to increase my breast from a cup to c cup with a lift. Can this be done with cysts
breast cysts
Aug 24, 2018
Many (?most) women have cysts in their breasts. It is incredibly common. If the cysts are very large they can cause deformity during breast augmentation. In general, they will be felt but not seen so yes, you can have an aug. 
Can you have a full abominoplasty done at the same time you have a panniculectomy?
It’s included
Aug 24, 2018
An abdominoplasty includes removing the pannus but also plication of the abdominal muscles and moving the umbilicus to its new position. It sounds like you are asking if insurance pays for an abdominoplasty and anyone performing an abdominoplasty but billing insurance for a panniculectomy would be committing fraud. 
Do I need to get implant removed & breast lift or just removal?
Removing implant
Aug 24, 2018
By only removing an implant without a lift and replacing the implant, you may end up with no superior fullness. It is not difficult to replace the implant at the time of mastopexy. I suggest this to all patients.
I’ve had 375cc over the muscle should I expect many changes like if you have them over the musc
subglandular implants
Aug 24, 2018
Some times, placing the implants over the muscle can give an attractive appearance to the breast but I’ve found over time, there is minimal superior fullness. If this is the look that you want, you will be happy at the end.
What kind of operation do you recommend for me? Tubular, underdevelopment, PCOS, degree of ptos
Ptosis
Aug 24, 2018
Your 2 photos appear very different but you do not appear to have tuberous breast deformity. You do have breast ptosis and are a candidate for a mastopexy with or without augmentation mammaplasty.
Do I need tubular breast augmentation?
Nope
Aug 24, 2018
You do not have tuberous breast deformity. The signs of tuberous breasts include: 1. a high inframammary fold, 2. constricted breasts, 3. herniation of the breast tissue into the areola. From your photo, you have none of these.
