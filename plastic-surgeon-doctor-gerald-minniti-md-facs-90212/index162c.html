Which size between 500cc and 550cc would best suit my body structure?
Sizing
Jun 30, 2020
Get sized by your surgeon using in office purpose build sizers and a sports bra. You will be able to see the difference, which is only a change of 10% of volume (50 cc). What’s more important is your nipple to fold distance on stretch. If it is not at least 10 cm, your nipple will either be too low on the breast mound or your surgeon will drop your natural fold too far, putting you at risk for double bubble.
