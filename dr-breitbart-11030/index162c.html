Will a tummy tuck get rid of pregnancy stretch marks?
Removing Stretch Marks with Tummy Tuck
Aug 21, 2019
During a standard tummy tuck, the skin between the belly button and the pubic area is excised, and the rest of the skin is pulled down.  If your stretch marks are within the area of the skin being removed, the stretch marks will be removed along with this skin.
What is a tummy tuck recovery like?
Tummy Tuck Recovery
Aug 21, 2019
I perform the vast majority of my tummy tucks as outpatient surgery.  Most patients are back to work within 1-2 weeks.  Generally, patients look and feel great in less than a month.
Can you have a baby after a tummy tuck?
Pregnancy after Tummy Tuck
Aug 21, 2019
In general, if you plan to get pregnant within the next few years, it may be best to delay the tummy tuck.  I have performed many tummy tucks though on weight loss patients, who subsequently became pregnant, and did just fine, as well as post-pregnancy patients who had another pregnancy after their tummy tuck.  In general, these patients ultimately still looked great, and did not require any tummy tuck revision.
How Long is the Recovery Period for Full Tummy Tuck?
Recovery Time after Tummy Tuck
Aug 21, 2019
Most patients are able to return to work 1-2 weeks after a full tummy tuck.  Depending on your work, this time may be longer or shorter.
Can you get a regular tummy tuck if you are 68 years old or is that considered too old?
Too Old for Tummy Tuck?
Aug 21, 2019
I have performed many tummy tucks on patients 68 years old and even older.  As long as you are medically healthy, and can get medical clearance from your internist, you can still be a good candidate for a tummy tuck, even at 68.
Silicone Breast Implants Safe?
Safety of Silicone Breast Implants
Aug 21, 2019
Silicone breast implants are considered safe, and have been FDA approved for many years.  Most patients today are choosing silicone gel breast implants.  The FDA does recommend monitoring the implants every few years with studies such as MRI or high resolution ultrasound, to make sure that the implants are intact and not ruptured.
How Long is Breast Implants Recovery Time?
Breast Implant Recovery Time
Aug 21, 2019
For most patients, the recovery time, in terms of going back to work, is a few days or less.  You should be limiting very strenuous activity for a couple weeks though.
How Long to Wear Compression Garment After Smart Lipo
SmartLipo and Compression Garments
Aug 21, 2019
For most of my patients, I recommend wearing the compression garment most of the time for the first 2 weeks after the SmartLipo, and then at least 12 hours a day for the next 2 weeks.
Liposuction Vs. SmartLipo - Which is Better?
SmartLipo vs. Traditional Liposuction
Aug 21, 2019
As with any procedure, the effectiveness can be related to exact procedure, as well as the experience of the surgeon.  I personally have been performing liposuction for over 25 years, and for over the past 10 years, virtually all my liposuction cases have been with using SmartLipo.  I have found that SmartLipo has many advantages over traditional liposuction, including more effective suctioning of fat, better results, and quicker recovery.
Will this procedure really help me scale down my thighs?
SmartLipo of Thighs
Aug 21, 2019
SmartLipo is a very effective liposuction technique to reduce the fat and get some skin tightening.  As with all liposuction techniques, it works best to treat localized areas of disproportionate excess fat.
I'm hoping Smart Lipo will help mother’s apron?
SmartLipo of Abdomen
Aug 21, 2019
SmartLipo will effectively remove the fat with liposuction and get some skin tightening.  However, the degree of skin laxity/apron that you have would necessitate a tummy tuck, or at least a mini-tummy tuck, to get substantial tightening.

Breast augmentation and breast lift

Can I get away with just breast augmentation without a breast lift?
Breast augmentation and breast lift
Apr 25, 2019
Based on your photos, it seems like you would benefit from a breast lift at the time of your breast augmentation.  There are minimal scar breast lift procedures which may be indicated in your case.

Nipple piercings and breast surgery
Apr 25, 2019
Nipple piercings should be removed prior to breast augmentation, both because of infection concerns and safety concerns with cautery devices that are often used in surgical procedures.
Is it normal to feel ripples at the base of the incision 4 months post-op?
Ripples after Breast Augmentation
Apr 25, 2019
Even with silicone implants, including those placed under the muscle. ripples can sometimes be felt at the bottom of the implant.  This is not necessarily abnormal, but you can be examined by your surgeon to make sure everything is okay.
