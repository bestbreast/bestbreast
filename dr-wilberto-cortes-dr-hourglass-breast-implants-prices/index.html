Breast implant sizing

The best way to determine the right size for you is to try on the different implants in a bra and under a fitted shirt. You can do this in your surgeon’s office.

There’s not a huge difference in the sizes you mentioned, but if you think you want to be slightly larger or smaller, you should definitely mention this to your surgeon.

Communication will ensure a happy patient and surgeon.

It is 600 cc too big?

This question is very difficult to answer without knowing your height, weight and measurements. In general a 600 cc implant is a very large implant. For a tall woman with broad shoulders it may be appropriate.

For smaller and petite woman it may look excessively top heavy. It’s hard for patients to go with numbers alone. I strongly recommend that you look at implant sizers on your frame. Think about how the implants balances with your hips, your shoulders, and your goals.

Uneven breast correction

I think the key in these situations is for you and your surgeon to have a good discussion about what the goals of the procedure are, and in cases of asymmetry, the goal is improved symmetry, not to create two identical breasts, since this is virtually impossible.

With that in mind, obtaining a more balanced look is certainly reasonable, and it involves different sized implants, and perhaps some component of a lift on one or both sides. It really depends on your starting point and what your surgeon feels can be done safely.

Breast implant sizing

Only an in person exam and measurements can say what is the best implant size for you. The key is NOT the final volume but rather choosing the PROFILE of the implant (ie moderate vs high profile) to create the “look” you want and then using the implant of that chosen profile in a diameter that well matches your chest diameter.

You can also try on this implant in a sports bra simulation to see if you like the basic size it gives you.

Breasts uneven

Breast asymmetry is normal. No one is perfectly symmetrical. If the difference in size is bothersome to you surgical intervention is an option. You could augment the smaller breast to match the larger, or reduce the larger to match the smaller.

Possible to Have Soft and Bouncy Breasts from Implants?

If you choose the right Plastic Surgeon who performs a natural breast augmentation you should be able to achieve your goal using silicone implants. In a natural augmentation the implants are not too big so there is some softness and movement.

Running shouldn’t affect your breast implants

Getting back into your normal exercise routine shouldn’t have a negative impact on your breast implants, but be sure to ease back into being active and don’t push yourself.

Be sure to invest in a high-quality sports bra to keep your breasts supported and comfortable while running.

This helps limit the strain that can occur on the supportive ligaments of the breast, which can help prevent excessive sagging. Finally, be sure you take your running habits into consideration when you’re choosing your breast implant size.
