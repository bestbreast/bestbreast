Why do some breasts have a square after a reduction?
Breast reduction scars and shapes
Jun 5, 2020
Dear tighhter,A significant contributor to the postoperative breast shape after breast reduction surgery is the technique that is employed.  In general, the inverted T or anchor incision technique creates a more boxy shaped breast.  This criticism of the technique was one of the driving forces behind development of vertical (lollipop scar) techniques, which, in general, create the more smooth, rounder breast shape.
