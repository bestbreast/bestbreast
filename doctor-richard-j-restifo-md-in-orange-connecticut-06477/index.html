What variables go into deciding what kind of incisions will be used for an individual's breast r
Breast Reduction Incisions
Nov 20, 2019
You are correct in your assumption that the more tissue that is removed and the more "lift" that is needed, the more skin needs to be removed and thus there will be a greater likelihood of needing an anchor scar pattern.  However, many experienced surgeons are able to perform substantial reductions and lifts with just a "lollipop" type of incision. Be sure to consult with a surgeon that has a lot of experience in "vertical mammaplasty" if your goal is to stay with a lollipop.  Best wishes.
Do you get a better result with an arm lift if you have liposuction at the same time?
Arm lift and liposuction
Aug 28, 2019
It is important to realize that the arm lift itself will remove far more fat than can be removed with liposuction, so in many arm lift cases it actually doesn't make sense to do liposuction of the same area. It is also important that you be comfortable with everything before moving forward, so getting a second opinion is not a bad idea.  But again it is not unusual for a surgeon to avoid liposuction during the course of an arm lift.  Best wishes.
Is there a required BMI for an arm lift?
Arm Lift and BMI
Aug 28, 2019
Although it recommended that a patient be at his or her best BMI prior to any elective cosmetic surgery, there is no absolute cutoff.  Based upon the photos it appears that there is excess skin and fat on your arms that could be treated with an arm lift.  It is important to consult with a surgeon that has a lot of experience with this operation.  Best wishes.
Could the lymphatic system as a whole be disrupted during a brachioplasty?
Lymphatics and Brachioplasty
Aug 28, 2019
The brachioplasty will often come close to the lymphatics so care must be exercised to avoid damaging them. Lymphedema after brachioplasty is a very rare complication, and in my experience cellutlis is very rare also.These are good questions to bring up with your plastic surgeon; ask him or her what the plan is to avoid disruption of the lymph system. Best wishes.
What can I do post arm lift surgery to minimize scaring?
Optimizing scars after brachioplasty.
Aug 28, 2019
Most plastic surgeons use some sort of evidence-based modality to help the scars heal as nicely as possible.  These usually involve silicone either as a sheet or an ointment, and some surgeons use methods to take tension off the closure as well, such as plastic films or tapes.  There are some combination products that use silicone and also reduce the tension at the skin closure. Your plastic surgeon should be able to discuss which modality he or she uses.  Best wishes.
