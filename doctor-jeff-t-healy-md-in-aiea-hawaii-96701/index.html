Should I wear a bra of some sort around the clock and Will bra type change my breast shape?
Bra after Breast Augmentation
Apr 20, 2019
We advise our patients to wear a sports bra for the first 6 weeks.  After that the patient is allowed to wear any bra that is comfortable.  A bra should not change the shape or settling of your implants as long as it is not too constrictive.  Always best to follow the advice of your surgeon. 
Is yellow skin at 2 weeks post op normal healing?

Apr 8, 2019
A tummy tuck is a big operation and there are different stages of healing that each patient will go through.  At 2 weeks you are still very early in the healing process.  The yellow color you see on your skin is most likely the last stages of the bruising as it fades away.  This is very normal.  You should always follow-up with your surgeon so he/she can assess the progress of your recovery.  Follow your surgeon's recommendations and you should have an uneventful recovery.

1 weeks post op breast augmentation, I can sometimes feel the implant move when my chest muscles flex
Implant moving with muscle activity
Feb 23, 2019
It is completely normal for your implants to move when the pectoralis muscles are activated.  Even small movements such as opening a door or raising your arms above your head will cause the muscles to flex.  Most implants are placed partially underneath the pectoralis muscles so the implants will move when the muscles move.
