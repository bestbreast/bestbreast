Antibiotics before twilight surgery for breast argumentation?
Sinus infection and breast augmentation
Jun 18, 2016
You should discuss this with your surgeon. Breast augmentation is an elective procedure and your physical condition should be excellent before surgery.  An active sinus infection is a relative contraindication for breast augmentation.  So, it may be that your will need to postpone your surgery to decrease your risk of complications.Best wishes,Dr. Connall

Breast fat grafting
Dec 23, 2015
Breast fat grafting is very common in my practice. It is considered a safe option for patients desiring a small change in size.
