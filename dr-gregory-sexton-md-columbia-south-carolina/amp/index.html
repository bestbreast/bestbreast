Why are implants placed under the muscle more often?
Breast augmentation
Jul 4, 2020
There are three reasons to put iMplants behind the muscle. Number one, it makes it easier on the radiologist to read the mammogram. Number two, it helps to hide the edges of the implant, especially when using saline. Number three, it reduces the risk Of capsular contracture Having said that, most surgeons will place it in either position and leave the choice to you.Good luck,Greg Sexton, M.
