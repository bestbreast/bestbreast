Breast Enlargement Surgery In Florida

There are 3 main options for the placement of breast implants in Florida:

– over the muscle (subpectoral)
– under the muscle (subglandular)
– half over/half under the muscle (dual plane).

Over the muscle allows for reduced recovery time, less discomfort and is an effective way to correct slight breast ptosis.

However, this technique tends to produce a less naural looking result and studies have shown leads to an increased risk of capsular contracture. Under the muscle significantly reduces that risk and allows for more natural looking results, particularly for patients with saline implants which can ocassionally show rippling if placed above the muscle.

The disadvantage to this technique is added discomfort and additional downtime, but patients are still able to recover within a few days and are prescribed medication to alleviate any discomfort. The preferred method for breast augmentation in FL is the Dual Plane technique (half over/half under).

This provides patients with the same benefits of the subpectoral technique, including a reduced risk of capsular contracture and a more natural look. Infact, this technique tends to provide the most natural looking results of all three options.
